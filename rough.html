L.J.Iyengar Bakery


      In 1982, L.J. IYENGAR'S Bakery(LJIB) opened its first small showroom
   in the city HUBLI opposite to convent high school. From its inception,LJIB has
   been cherished for its classic baked goods, vintage decor and warm,
   inviting atmosphere.
      Few years later, LJIB original, the owner has thoughtfully expanded the bakery from its
   first small shop to its current five branches all over the city. Starting
   with  the firewood then the baakery slowly  started to develop by using more advanced
   machinery like electric oven and later diesel oven and other advanced machineries;
   Now you can see LJIB in many parts of the city like keshwapur,madhura colony,vidyanagar, and in other
   parts.Specialized in bakery and bakery products
