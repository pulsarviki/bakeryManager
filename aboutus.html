WELCOME TO L.J IYENGAR BAKERY










L.J.Iyengar Bakery


      In 1996, Magnolia Bakery opened its first location on a quiet street corner
   in the heart of New York’s West Village. From its inception,Magnolia has
   been cherished for its classic American baked goods, vintage decor and warm,
   inviting atmosphere.
      In 2007, Magnolia’s original owner passed her oven mitts to Steve and Tyra
   Abrams. Together, the Abrams’ have thoughtfully expanded the bakery from its
   first West Village shop to its current eight locations worldwide. Today,
   Magnolia can be found on Columbus Avenue, in Rockefeller Center, Bloomingdale’s
   flagship and Grand Central Station in New York; West ThirdStreet in Los Angeles;
   Block 37 in Chicago; in The Dubai Mall in the United Arab Emirates; Dar Al
   Salem Mall in Doha, Qatar; in the Al Hamra Tower in Kuwait City, Kuwait;
   and in ABC Dbayeh Mall in Beirut, Lebanon.
